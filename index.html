Projects Resume

Hello! I'm Andrés Guerrero, a digital ux designer living in sunny Florida.

Pharmacy Gdl.

Pharmacy Guadalajara is a 70-years-old pharmacy store chain looking to keep competitive by embracing a digital transformation. With a strong focus on improving the experience delivered on digital channels, we designed a new eCommerce where I helped design the interaction for mobile devices.

Interjet airline

Interjet is a Mexican low-cost business model airline that is well-positioned as a referent of great service and growing offering of destinations; nevertheless, poor user experience affects business and customer goals. Redesigning eCommerce and creating a UX team was my contribution to the project.

Liverpool eCommerce

Liverpool is the biggest department store chain in Mexico that has gotten to keep growing and evolving in a highly competitive eCommerce environment. I had the opportunity to manage the digital experience and create and lead its first UX team.
